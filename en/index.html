Origin

October 2022. Pablo launched Douf, his own clothing brand. First drop: 40 orders in six weeks, six countries, zero euros in advertising. The second fell 76%.

He tried to capitalize on the success before understanding what had produced it. That is where he learned the rule he applies to every account today:

"If you capitalize on a success before understanding what produced it, you kill it."

That is why he founded DayByDay with Jorge. Before moving a lever: stop, look, understand what works. Then execute.
